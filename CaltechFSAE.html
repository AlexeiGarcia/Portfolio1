Caltech Electrical Vehicle Formula One Team

I am a new member of the Caltech Racing team that is working between the mechanical and electrical leads. This current year, the FSAE team is entirely redesigning the car design.

My current task is designing the electrical packaging for the motor controller board that integrates a water bed and fan as the coolant system. Through the club I am learning how to design 3D models on solidworks and electrical circuits on CircuitMaker as well as a wide variety of other practical skills.
