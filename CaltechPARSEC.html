Caltech PARSEC Rocketry Team

I am a new member of the Caltech Rocketry team that is working in both the avionics and propulsion teams. PARSEC is a brand new student led organization in Caltech that started up in the summer of 2017. The current year will be characterized by research and design of a rocket test stand and phsycical rocket for the FAR-MARS rocketry competition.

Read more about the competition here: http://friendsofamateurrocketry.org/Launch_Contest.html

My current task is designing the electrical sensor boards for the rocket test stand that we plan to have built by the beginning of 2018. A full rocket is scheduled to be built, tested, and launched at competition by 2019.
